Character Design Project

The following images were from project where two characters needed to be designed in a way where they logically fit in a world together, in both style and content.

The character design pages have a simple turn-around and silhouette, while the action page is comparing their personalities.

Unicorn Illustrations Project

The following images was from a project where a variety of unicorns needed to be illsutrated in a way that made them feel like they were drawn in a scientist's field journal while studying them.

The unicorns needed to feel like distinctive breeds from one another, with different body shapes and coloration.
